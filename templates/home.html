Preparation Goes A Long Way

I love the fact that the great outdoors has never been more accessible. As technology progresses, we inherit information and amazing tools that provide us with the security of knowledge and the confidence to tackle any challenge. I find that there are ample resources for the discovery of hiking destinations wherever you may visit. Any avid traveller can find joy at the mere prospect of planning their escape to some of the most beautiful places in the world.

I value preparation as much as anyone, but like branches on a trail, plans can can snap given enough stress. Mistakes are to be made, and unfortunately for hikers, the solutions may be out of reach. I find myself with a good sense of direction on the trail, but put me on the road and I am ingrained to seek the instruction of mapping software from my phone. When you're going to your hike, the connection is there and you can map your way. But what happens when you're heading back home? Alas, middle-of-nowhere hiking destinations are notorious for being poor providers of cell reception. In this situation, you realize that you're desperate for some sort of clue, some sort of resource to help you gather your bearings.

Have Directions On Hand

TrekAssure is designed to generate a neatly packed "After-The-Hike" Pamphlet that contains a static set of directions home and to a few key emergency locations nearest to the trail of your choosing. You can download the pamphlet as a text file and email a copy to yourself. By adding TrekAssure to your preparation routine before a hike, you can be sure that you'll have a hard copy set of directions that you can access without a connection.

Once you're on your way home, if you find cell reception, use it! Get home quickly and safely. All the better if you've had connection the whole time! However, for those situations when you are deep in the mountains and you have no idea where you are, you'll be glad to have your pamphlet to at least get you going in the right direction. Like jumper cables, you never appreciate your contingency measures until you need them. Use TrekAssure before the hike to prepare for the journey back home. Make sure your day of hiking ends on a great, stress-free note!

TrekAssure is powered by MapQuest's Developer API and all trail information is acquired from the excellent Hiking Project API. TrekAssure is designed for the continental US. Results for international users may not be optimal. This is a project created by Nathan Bryant (LinkedIn).
